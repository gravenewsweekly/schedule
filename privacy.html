Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **Simple Scheduling App** collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using this website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Username, email, and password (encrypted).
  • Usage Data: Website interactions, IP address, and device type.
  • Schedule Data: Subjects and time schedules you create.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Providing access to your account and schedules.
  • Improving website performance and user experience.
  • Ensuring website security and fraud prevention.

3. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of online storage is **100% secure**. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

4. Third-Party Services

We **do not sell or share** your personal information with third parties. However, we may use trusted third-party tools for website analytics (such as Google Analytics) that collect non-personal data.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Compliance with Indian Laws

We follow **Indian legal standards**, including:

  • Information Technology (IT) Act, 2000: Regulates digital data protection.
  • Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB), 2019: Governs how personal data is stored.

7. Compliance with International Laws

We also comply with global privacy laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – EU: Users have the right to request data deletion.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) – USA: Users can opt-out of data collection.
  • Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) – USA: No data is collected from users under 13.

8. User Rights & Data Control

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your stored data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time.

9. Data Retention Policy

We retain user data **only as long as necessary** for website operations. If an account is inactive for 12 months, it will be deleted automatically.

10. Data Breach & Notification

In case of a data breach, we will:

  • Investigate and secure the affected system.
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours.
  • Report serious breaches to the relevant authorities.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy **without prior notice**. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the latest version.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at **privacy@simpleschedule.com**.
